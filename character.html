Characters

Main Characters

Naruto Uzumaki

The Nine-Tails Jinchūriki who, through determination and courage, grows into the hero of the Hidden Leaf and fulfills his dream of becoming Hokage.

Sasuke Uchiha

Last survivor of the Uchiha clan massacre. Seeks power to avenge his family and later redemption for his dark path.

Sakura Haruno

Talented medical ninja and member of Team 7. Student of Tsunade with incredible strength and healing abilities.

Kakashi Hatake

The Copy Ninja and Sixth Hokage. Former ANBU captain who leads Team 7 and possesses the Sharingan.

All Characters

A (Third Raikage)

The strongest Raikage in history, known for his incredible taijutsu and Lightning Release armor. Died protecting his village.

A (Fourth Raikage)

Leader of the Hidden Cloud Village known for his incredible speed and Lightning Release chakra mode. Killer B's adoptive brother.

Abiru

A shinobi from the Hidden Rock Village who participated in various missions during the ninja wars.

Agari Kaisen

A skilled ninja known for his tactical abilities and service to his village during wartime.

Akamaru

Kiba Inuzuka's loyal canine companion. A ninken who fights alongside his partner using combination techniques.

Akatsuchi

A large shinobi from Iwagakure and bodyguard to the Third Tsuchikage Onoki. Master of Earth Release techniques.

Amachi

A former ninja turned scientist who conducted forbidden experiments on sea creatures and chakra enhancement.

Anko Mitarashi

Former student of Orochimaru and proctor of the Chunin Exams. Bears the Cursed Seal of Heaven on her neck.

Ao

Sensor ninja from the Hidden Mist with a transplanted Byakugan. Loyal bodyguard to the Fifth Mizukage.

Arashi

A skilled shinobi known for his wind-based techniques and service during the Great Ninja Wars.

Araya

A Sand Village ninja specializing in puppet techniques and desert warfare tactics.

Asuma Sarutobi

Son of the Third Hokage and sensei to Team 10. Uses chakra blades and has Wind Release nature. Father of Mirai.

Ayame

Daughter of Teuchi and waitress at Ichiraku Ramen. She is kind, cheerful, and always treats Naruto warmly as a valued customer and friend.

Baki

Jonin from the Hidden Sand Village and former sensei to Gaara, Temari, and Kankuro. Wind Release specialist.

Baiu

A shinobi known for his lightning-based techniques and participation in various village missions.

Biwako Sarutobi

Wife of the Third Hokage Hiruzen and skilled medical ninja who assisted in Naruto's birth.

Black Zetsu

Manifestation of Kaguya Otsutsuki's will, manipulating events for centuries to revive his creator.

C (Shi)

A Cloud Village ninja and assistant to the Fourth Raikage, specializing in sensory techniques and intelligence.

Chiyo

Legendary puppet master and medical ninja from the Sand Village. Grandmother of Sasori and creator of many puppets.

Chojuro

Sixth Mizukage and one of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen. Wields the twin swords Hiramekarei with great skill.

Choji Akimichi

Kind-hearted member of the Akimichi clan with body expansion techniques. Values friendship above all else.

Darui

Fifth Raikage and former bodyguard to the Fourth Raikage. Master of Lightning and Water Release techniques.

Deidara

Artist-ninja from Iwagakure who considers explosions as art. Uses clay infused with chakra to create explosive sculptures.

Dosu Kinuta

Sound ninja who uses sound-based attacks through his Melody Arm device. Participated in the Chunin Exams.

Ebisu

Elite tutor known as the Closet Pervert. Specializes in training young ninja from prestigious clans.

Ebizo

Elder of the Hidden Sand Village and brother to Chiyo. Skilled in puppet techniques and village politics.

Fū (Seven-Tails Jinchuriki)

Cheerful ninja from Takigakure and jinchuriki of the Seven-Tails. Can fly using her tailed beast's abilities.

Fugaku Uchiha

Former head of the Uchiha clan and father to Itachi and Sasuke. Possessed the Mangekyo Sharingan.

Gaara

Fifth Kazekage and former One-Tail Jinchuriki. Once feared as a monster, he became a beloved leader through Naruto's influence.

Gengetsu Hozuki (Second Mizukage)

Second leader of the Hidden Mist Village known for his water techniques and rivalry with the Second Tsuchikage.

Ginkaku

One of the Gold and Silver Brothers from the Cloud Village who ate Nine-Tails chakra and gained its power.

Guren

Former follower of Orochimaru with the unique Crystal Release kekkei genkai. Becomes protective of the boy Yukimaru.

Haku

Zabuza's loyal companion with the rare Ice Release kekkei genkai. Lives only to serve his master's dreams.

Hamura Otsutsuki

Younger son of Kaguya Otsutsuki and ancestor of the Hyuga clan. Helped seal the Ten-Tails with his brother.

Han (Five-Tails Jinchuriki)

Jinchuriki of the Five-Tails from Iwagakure. Wears steam-powered armor and uses boil-based techniques.

Hanabi Hyuga

Hinata's younger sister and heir to the Hyuga clan. Talented user of the Byakugan and Gentle Fist style.

Hanzo of the Salamander

Legendary leader of the Hidden Rain Village who gave the Sannin their title. Known for his poisonous salamander.

Hashirama Senju (First Hokage)

The God of Shinobi who founded Konoha with Madara. Master of Wood Release and the most powerful ninja of his era.

Hayate Gekko

Sickly proctor of the Chunin Exams preliminary rounds. Master of the Moonlight sword technique.

Hidan

Immortal Akatsuki member who worships the god Jashin. Uses a ritual scythe and blood-based curse techniques.

Hiashi Hyuga

Head of the Hyuga clan and father to Hinata and Hanabi. Master of the Byakugan and Gentle Fist techniques.

Hiruzen Sarutobi (Third Hokage)

The Professor who knew every jutsu in Konoha. Naruto's father figure and mentor to the Legendary Sannin.

Hizashi Hyuga

Hiashi's twin brother from the branch family of the Hyuga clan. Neji's father who died to protect the main family.

Homura Mitokado

Elder advisor of Konoha and former teammate of the Third Hokage. Part of the village council.

Ibiki Morino

Head of Konoha's Torture and Interrogation Force. Proctor of the written portion of the Chunin Exams.

Ino Yamanaka

Mind-transfer specialist from the Yamanaka clan. Skilled in reconnaissance and mental invasion techniques.

Inoichi Yamanaka

Head of the Yamanaka clan and Ino's father. Expert in mind-reading and interrogation techniques.

Iruka Umino

Academy instructor and Naruto's first father figure. Lost his parents to the Nine-Tails attack but still cared for Naruto.

Itachi Uchiha

Sasuke's older brother who massacred the Uchiha clan to prevent a civil war. A genius ninja who sacrificed everything for peace.

Jinin Akebino

One of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist who wielded the explosive blade Shibuki.

Jiraiya

The Toad Sage and one of the Legendary Sannin. Naruto's mentor and author of the Icha Icha series.

Jugo

Member of Sasuke's team Taka with the ability to absorb natural energy. Source of the Cursed Seal power.

Kabuto Yakushi

Former spy and Orochimaru's right hand who later becomes a sage. Master of medical ninjutsu and Edo Tensei.

Kankuro

Puppet master ninja and Gaara's brother. Uses chakra strings to control deadly puppets in combat.

Karui

Hot-tempered kunoichi from the Hidden Cloud Village and teammate of Omoi. Later marries Choji Akimichi.

Karin

Member of Sasuke's team Taka with powerful sensory abilities and healing powers through chakra absorption.

Kiba Inuzuka

Beast-like ninja who fights alongside his canine companion Akamaru using combination attacks and enhanced senses.

Killer B

Eight-Tails Jinchuriki and rap-loving ninja from the Hidden Cloud. Perfect Jinchuriki who befriends his tailed beast.

Kimimaro

Last member of the Kaguya clan with the bone-manipulating kekkei genkai. Orochimaru's most loyal follower.

Kisame Hoshigaki

The Monster of the Hidden Mist and Itachi's partner. Wields the legendary sword Samehada and has massive chakra reserves.

Konan

The Angel of Amegakure and only female member of the original Akatsuki. Uses paper-based ninjutsu techniques.

Konohamaru Sarutobi

Grandson of the Third Hokage who looks up to Naruto as his rival and mentor. Later becomes a jonin sensei.

Kurenai Yuhi

Genjutsu specialist and sensei to Team 8. Mother of Mirai Sarutobi and former lover of Asuma.

Kushina Uzumaki

Former Nine-Tails Jinchuriki and Naruto's mother. Known as the Red Hot-Blooded Habanero for her fiery personality.

Madara Uchiha

Legendary Uchiha leader and co-founder of Konoha. The main antagonist who seeks to cast the world in an eternal genjutsu.

Mangetsu Hozuki

Suigetsu's older brother and member of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen. Could wield all seven swords of the Mist.

Mei Terumi (Fifth Mizukage)

Leader of the Hidden Mist Village with unique Lava and Boil Release abilities. Helped end the Bloody Mist era.

Mifune

General of the Land of Iron and leader of the samurai. Neutral mediator who survived an encounter with Hanzo.

Mikoto Uchiha

Mother of Itachi and Sasuke, wife of Fugaku. A kind woman who was killed during the Uchiha massacre.

Minato Namikaze (Fourth Hokage)

The Yellow Flash of Konoha and Naruto's father. Master of the Flying Thunder God technique and hero of the Third Great Ninja War.

Moegi Kazamatsuri

Former member of the Konohamaru Corps who later becomes a jonin sensei. Develops Wood Release abilities.

Mu (Second Tsuchikage)

Second leader of Iwagakure known for his Particle Style kekkei genkai and ability to become invisible.

Nagato (Pain)

Nawaki Senju

Tsunade's younger brother who dreamed of becoming Hokage. Died young during the Second Great Ninja War.

Neji Hyuga

Hyuga clan prodigy who overcame the fate of the branch family. Master of the Byakugan and Gentle Fist fighting style.

Nii Yugito (Two-Tails Jinchuriki)

Jonin from the Hidden Cloud Village and jinchuriki of the Two-Tails cat demon, Matatabi.

Obito Uchiha

Kakashi's former teammate who became the masked man manipulating events from the shadows. Seeks to create a perfect world.

Onoki (Third Tsuchikage)

The Fence Sitter and leader of Iwagakure. Master of Particle Style and one of the longest-serving Kage.

Orochimaru

One of the Legendary Sannin who seeks immortality and forbidden knowledge. Master of snake techniques and body modification.

Pakura

Hero of the Hidden Sand Village with the unique Scorch Release kekkei genkai. Betrayed by her own village.

Rin Nohara

Medical ninja and teammate of Kakashi and Obito. Her death became Obito's motivation for his villainous path.

Rock Lee

Taijutsu specialist who can't use ninjutsu or genjutsu. Proves that hard work can overcome natural talent through dedication.

Sai

Sakon & Ukon

Head of the Aburame clan and Shino's father. Master of insect-based techniques and surveillance.

Shikaku Nara

Head of the Nara clan and Shikamaru's father. Chief strategist of Konoha with incredible tactical intelligence.

Shikamaru Nara

Suigetsu Hozuki

Member of Team Taka with the ability to liquefy his body. Dreams of collecting all the swords of the Seven Swordsmen.

Tonbei

Torune Aburame
