JUTSU'S ENCYCLOPEDIA

Clone Technique

Name: 分身の術 (Bunshin no Jutsu – Clone Technique)

Type: Ninjutsu (Basic)

Rank: E

Element: None

Users: All academy students (Naruto, Sasuke, Sakura, etc.)

Village: All villages

Lore: A basic illusionary technique that creates intangible copies of the user. Though ineffective in real combat, it is the first step in molding chakra.

Transformation Technique

Name: 変化の術 (Henge no Jutsu – Transformation Technique)

Type: Ninjutsu (Basic)

Rank: E

Element: None

Users: All academy students (Naruto, Konohamaru Corps, etc.)

Village: All villages

Lore: The art of disguising oneself as another person, object, or illusion. Vital for espionage, though Naruto often used it for pranks.

Substitution Technique

Name: 変わり身の術 (Kawarimi no Jutsu – Substitution Technique)

Type: Ninjutsu (Basic)

Rank: E

Element: None

Users: Most shinobi (Naruto, Kakashi, Sasuke, etc.)

Village: All villages

Lore: The user switches themselves with a nearby object like a log or animal to evade attacks. A lifesaving trick mastered early by shinobi.

Body Flicker Technique

Name: 瞬身の術 (Shunshin no Jutsu – Body Flicker Technique)

Type: Ninjutsu (Movement)

Rank: D–B

Element: Varies by user

Users: Kakashi, Itachi, Shisui, many jonin

Village: All villages

Lore: By infusing chakra into their body, shinobi can move at blinding speeds. Shisui mastered it so well he was feared as 'Shisui of the Body Flicker'.
